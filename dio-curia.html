Chancery:

The Chancellor of the Diocese of Banmaw is the Executive Secretary of the General Secretariat of the Diocese. He gives secretarial service to Bishop and Vicar General, executes their mandates and help in the administrative affairs of the Diocese. He is also the official notary on all of the documents issued by the diocesan bishop.

Our Office

- What we do -

Administrative Affairs:

Prepare, gather, and arrange the Administrative Acts of the Diocese and duly dissement them.

Record & Safeguard:

Orderly register sacramental records, Admin Acts and other documents, and make them accessibe with proper permission.

Coordination:

Coordinate in between with other communities, within or outside of the Diocese, and share information.

History & Archives:

Trace back historical events of the diocese, keep on recording current diocesan events, and keep them in Dicoesan Archives.
