Banmaw Parish

Parish General Info

Brief Description

It is the Cathedral Parish of Banmaw Diocese located in Banmaw Township and on the highway road from Mandalay to Myitkyina. Since the creation of Banmaw Diocese it was administered by former Parish Priest, Fr. John Lahtaw Zau Doi. In last year, 2019, Rev. Fr. John Jangma Yung Was was assigned as the Parish Priest.

Cathedral

St. Patrick's Cathedral is the principal Church for the Parish as well the Cathedral for the Diocese, which was built by Columban Missionaries in the year of 1960.

Parish Info:

About 990 christian families with 5466 parishioners are sharing faith in Momauk Parish. It includes 17 villages with 25 Catechists.

Pastors

Rev. Fr. Yung Wa is the parish priest of Banmaw as well as the Vicar General of the Diocese. Formerly he was assigned to Mansi and Zau Bung parishes.
