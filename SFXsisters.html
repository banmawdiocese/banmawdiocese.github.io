Religious Institutes and Communities

Sisters of St. Francis Xavier

Sisters of St. Francis Xavier started their mission in the Diocese of Banmaw in 1990. At present, there are four Communities with (15) SFX Sisters working in different parishes and offices.
Each Community has its own Vission & Mission statements for the members.

ST. FRANCIS XAVIER CONVENT

Banmaw SFX Community

VISSION:

".. God’s presence known and felt by uplifting the dignity of all people .."

MISSION:

† Deeping the experience of God in the Consecrated Religious Life.
† Sharing the love of God both in Social and Pastoral works.
† Strengthening the community life by promoting Congregational Governance’s capacity in accordance with the sings of the time .

OBJECTIVE:

Well-formed and trained holistic and integrated Christ-like religious life to be ready and available to the call of the missions in any conditions.

ST. FRANCIS XAVIER CONVENT

Shwegu SFX Community

VISSION:

“.. God’s presence known and felt by uplifting the dignity of all people..”

MISSION:

† To develop faith by teaching catechism and preaching the Good news;
† To develop the lives of people by giving Education;
† To develop our relationship in community by sharing faith and life experiences and giving attention to community meetings, personal and professional development;

OBJECTIVE:

Cooperation in the propagation of the Catholic Faith through our apostolates.
