Vision & Mission Statement
  1. Our Inspiration - “That they may have life and have it abundantly” (Jn 10:10)
  2. Our Vision (Dream) - The church that is a united and participatory people of God, working together with faith, hope and love to build God’s kingdom of peace and justice.
  3. Our Mission (Goal) -  The Diocese of Banmaw seeks to empower as many people as possible in becoming fully developing followers of Christ.
