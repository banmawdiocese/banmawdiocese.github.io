SISTERS OF FRANCISCAN MISSIONARY OF MARY

Religious Institutes and Communities

Sisters of Franciscan Missionary of Mary

The Sisters of Franciscan Missionary of Mary started their mission at Prang Hkudung with their first arrival in 1923. At present, there are five Communities with 35 FMM Sisters living and working in different parishes and offices.
And each Community has its own Vission & Mission statements for the Community.

OUR LADY OF THE HOLY ROSARY CONVENT

Momauk FMM Community

VISSION:

" Living dialogue of life and collaborate with the local Church."

MISSION:

1) “ Living dialogue of life”

Priorities:
The Lord calls us to live in Gospel fraternity. His love gathers us together and forges bonds of a unity among us. As a living cell of the Church, the community is at the service of Evangelization.  The spirit is at work in the community, disposing us to receive the word, to decern the father’s will and to live out our offering following in Christ’s steps. (cont. Art. 19)

Lines of Action :
• To contemplate on the work of Goal daily to deep our Franciscan Spirituality.
• To create an atmosphere of silence to help us live the Contemplative community in mission.
• To have dialogue of life to promote mutual accompaniment with love and respect.

2) “Collaboration with the Local Church"

Priorities:
Like Christ we want to approach everyone with a humble heart, ready to learn and to receive as much as to give with his attitude of respect, acceptance of persons, openness and service. (Const. Art. 39)

Lines of Action:
• To live an eco-friendly life in the community and to conscientize others, in our mission.
• To move out from our comfort zones and give our time to different kinds of poor and Charitable works.
• We will prepare ourselves for various ministries.

Objective: To act-justly, to love tenderly and to walk humbly with our God. (Mic 6:8)

OBJECTIVES:

As Franciscan Contemplative Missionaries, we allow ourselves to grow in inner freedom and to witness to the Compassion and joy of the Gospel among ourselves and in our solidarity with the poor and the wounded planet.
