Our Diocese

The Creation of Banmaw Diocese

As the mission territory of Myitkyina Diocese was far distant and remote, and the number of believers was rapidly increasing, another new Diocese was prepared to be established soon. On August 28, 2006, Pope Benedict XVI created a new Diocese of Banmaw Suffragan to the Archdiocese of Mandalay and appointed Msgr. Raymond Sumlut Gam as the first Bishop of the Diocese. On November 17, 2006, the new Diocese was blessed and on the 18th of the month of November 2006, the new Bishop of Banmaw, Msgr. Raymond Sumlut Gam was ordained and enthroned by His Grace Archbishop Salvatore Pennacchio, the Apostolate Delegate to Myanmar.
