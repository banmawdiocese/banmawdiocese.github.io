Brief Description

Panghkak parish

At present, Panghkak parish is under the care of Rev. Fr. Paul Lahpai Awng Dang who was assigned as the parish priest on April 23, 2019. Three religious Sisters of St. Francis Xavier are also helping the priest and working for the parish.

Principal Church

Christ the King Church is the principal Church at Laiza Parish which was built by Rev. Fr. Joseph Nbwi Naw and blessed in the year of 2005.

Statistic:

About (777) Catholic families with (3,849) parishioners are living and sharing christian faith in Laiza Parish, and (24) catechists are helping parish priest in (11) communities.

Our Pastor

Rev. Fr. Paul Lahpai Awng Dang was ordined on March 29, 2006 and firstly assigned as the Parish Priest of Namlimpa Parish, and then, to Mansi Parish in 2011.
