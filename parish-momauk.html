Momauk Parish

Parish General Info

Brief Description

Momauk Parish is located ten miles away from Bhamo town, and it is one of four administrative townships. Created upon the arrival of a Columban Missionary Priest, Fr. Patrick Usher who started the mission in 1943 with only six families, Momauk Parish is one of the ordest local church in the Diocese. At present, Rev. Fr. Stephen Zaw Min Latt, the Parish Priest, with the help of Rev. Fr. John Zau Yaw and together with FMM Sisters and SFX Brothers, is taking care of its administration and pastoral services.

Principal Church

St. Patrick's Church is the principal Church for the Parish, and was built by former parish priest, Rev. Fr. Joseph Laban Tu, in the year of 2009.

Parish Info:

About 724 catholic families with 3829 parishioners are living and sharing christian faith in Momauk Parish. It has 9 communities with 23 Catechists.

Our Pastors
