Brief Description of Ting Sing Parish

The earliest priest who came to the area around Ting Sing and started the mission was MEP missionary, Rev. Fr. Herr, in 1929. It was written that he came from Shwe Bo that was part of Mandalay Diocese located in middle Myanmar. And later, Rev. Fr. Callard and Fr. Cassan joined the mission and laid faith foundation for the region. After handing over of the mission from MEP to Columban Missionaries, Rev. Fr. Lawrence McMahon (1950-1971) took charge of Ting Sing Parish. He built churches for the faithful in Ting Sing Pa, Hka U, Gat Rawn, Lung Hkat, Lai Law, Hka Nawng and Hka Pra villages.

At present, Ting Sing Parish is under the adminstration of Rev. Fr. Peter Chyauhpa Sunday Awng who was appointed as the parish priest on April 1, 2020.

Principal Church

St. Joseph's Church is the principal Church at Ting Sing Parish which was built by Rev. Fr. John Jangma Yung Wa and blessed in the year of 2019.
