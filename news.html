LAIZA / February 21, 2020

San Francis Xavier Mama Jawng Nta Ningnan

2020 ning February shata praw (21) bat hpang manga ya shani jahpawt 8:00 AM aten hta Mama jawng nta ningnan hpe Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang Up Wa Saradaw Raymond kaw nna lairi rep, shaman ntsin lapri let nta ningnan shaman poi Missa hkungga kaba hpe hpang wa sai. Wa Saradaw hte rau Laiza Jau Ginwang jawng dung Wajau Peter Lazum Tu Lum, Manmaw Jau Ginwang ningtau Wajau Columban Lar Di, Mainghkat Jau Ginwang jawng dung Wajau Lung Wa, Wajau John Zau Doi, Wajau Philip Lahpai Naw Ring, Deacon Tu Shan, San Francis Xavier Brother (2), Brother Gam Hpang, Myitkyina Saradaw Ginwang kaw nna sadu shanglawm ai Nu Mama (10) jan, Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang kaw na Nu Mama (25) hte rau poi sa hpung masha (200) darem ma sadu shanglawm masai.

BANMAW / February 25-26, 2020

Saradaw Ginwang Amyu Shayi ni a Zuphpawng kaba

2020 ning February shata 25-26 ya shani hta Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang Amyu shayi hpung ni a shawng lam masing hte seng ang ai zuphpawng kaba hpe Jau nta zuphpawng gawk hta galaw lai wa sai. Ndai zuphpawng kaba hpe atsawm rai ngut kre ai du hkra galaw karum ya ai ni gaw: Wajau Francis Saw Phay Han, Wajau Stephen Zaw Myin Latt, Shy. Lenette, Shd. Michael, Slg. Naw Din hte KMSS-BANMAW kaw nna marai mali ma shanglawm garum sai. Zuphpawng hta Jau Ginwang shagu na Amyu shayi adat kasa marai lahkawng hte lak san saw shaga da ai hpaji jaw salang ni hte nga yang yawng marai (50) jan rai nga ai.

BANMAW / May 30, 2020

Saradaw Ginwang Kunhting Jum galai ai lam

2020 ning May shata praw 30 ya jahpawt hta Wa Saradaw Raymond Sumlut Gam hku nna woi awn shang lawm galaw ai Missa hkungga ngut ai hte rau Saradaw ginwang kunhting jum ai lit magam galai ai lamang kadun hpe galaw lai wa sai. Ndai lamang hta nnan e Saradaw ginwang kunhting jum ai Wajau Noel Relly Dawla shara hta lai wa sai aten Laiza Jau Aseng ningtau jau lit magam hpe gun hpai ai Wajau Matthew Mwihpu Grawng Naw hku nna Saradaw, Missa hta shang lawm ai jau manang ni a man e tara shang matsing laika pa hta myit hkrum ai masat masa hku ta masat seng htu sai.

May 30, 2020

Oath of Fidelity & Office before taking Diocesan Finance Office

BANMAW / May 23, 2020

Saradaw Ginwang Ramma Ningbaw galai ai lam

2020 ning May shata praw 23 ya shani hta Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang Up Saradaw a malai Jau Kaba John Jangma Yung Wa hku nna woi awn shang lawm let Saradaw Ginwang Ramma Ningbaw galai ai lam lamang hpe galaw la lu sai. Lai wa sai aten hta Manmaw Jau Aseng ningtau jau lit magam hte Saradaw ginwang Ramma ningbaw galaw lai wa sai Wajau Columban Lar Di kaw nna ya aten Manmaw Jau Aseng ningtau jau lit magam gun hpai taw nga ai Wajau Francis Maran Awng Seng Naw hpe ndai lamang hta sa du shang lawm ai Jau Aseng shagu de na Ramma ningbaw, Jau, Mama ni marai (11) a man e lit magam hpe tara shang ndau shatsam up jaw ya sai.

San Joseph Shalat Jawng Yu Poi:

2020 ning March shata praw 19 ya, San Joseph a poi nhtoi jahpawt 6:30 AM aten hta Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang deng (2) Aung Tar lawk hta hpaw hpang shagrin da ai San Joseph shalat jawng a 27th lang na jawng yu poi Missa hkungga kaba hpe San Joseph Shalat Jawng Director Wajau Peter Sunday Awng, Shalat Jawng yu na Ramma (11) hte naw matut lung ra ai jawng ma (7) yawng (18), Manmaw Jau Aseng Up Wajau John Jangma Yung Wa, Zau Bung Jau Aseng Up Wajau Lazum La Di, Wajau Joseph Gawlu Gam, Wajau Joseph Awng Li, Wajau Joseph Labang Tu, Wajau Philip Lahpai Naw Ring, Momauk Jau Aseng Up Wajau Stephen Zaw Min Lat, Nu Mama (7), Brother (6) hte poi sa masha (20) ma sa shanglawm n-gun jaw la masai.

Jau htawt sit ai lamang (Tingsing Jau Aseng) 

Manmaw Saradaw Ginwang kata hta ra ahkrak ladawn ai lam ni nga ai majaw, Tingsing Jau Aseng Lana Zup Ja Yang mare San Luka nawku htingnu e 2020 ning May shata praw 3 ya jahpawt 10:00 AM hta jau htawt sit ai Missa hkungga hpe galaw hpang nna, 11:30 AM hta ngut ai hte rau jau ningnan hkalum la ai lamang ni hpe matut manoi galaw la lu sai. Tingsing Jau Aseng up jawng dung Wajau John Zau Yaw a shara hta ningnan e San Joseph shalat jawng up Wajau Sunday Awng hku nna jau ginwang up aya hpe matut gunhpai na matu ga sadi hkam la sai. Wajau Yung Wa, Wajau Michael Lung Wa, Wajau Columban Lar Di hte Tarahkaw Sara, Mungbawng hpung, Brother Zau Doi hte hpung masha (50) daram ma sa shanglawm sai.

Jau htawt sit ai lamang (Prang Hkudung Jau Aseng)

2020 ning May shata praw 5 ya jahpawt 7:00 AM aten e Prang Hkudung Jau Aseng, N-hkawng Pa mare San Peter nawku jawng hta Jau htawt sit ai Missa hkungga lamang galaw hpang nna 8:30 AM hta atsawm rai sha shangut la lu sai. N-dai lamang hta ningnan e Manmaw Jau Aseng ningtau jau lit magam hpe ngun hpai lai wa sai Wajau Columban Lar Di kaw nna jau ginwang up aya hpe ya htawt sit ra ai Wajau Peter San Tawng shara hta matut gun hpai na matu ga sadi hpe Wajau John Jangma Yung Wa, Brother (2), Nu Mama (2), hpung masha (100) jan a shawng man e hkap la sai.

Sarama Maria Lahpai Ja Bawk a Karai magam gun (50) ning hpring Ja Jubile:

Momauk Jau Ginwang, Chya Hkan Dap mare, San Yoseph nawku jawng hta Saranum Maria Lahpai Ja Bawk a Karai magam gun (50) ning hpring Ja Jubilee Missa hkungga hpe 2020 ning January shata praw 5 ya Hkawhkam Masum poi n-htoi, laban jahpawt 10:00 AM tup hta galaw hpang la nna shagrau lamang ni hte rau 1:00 PM hta shangut la lu sai. Momauk Jau Ginwang Up Wajau Zaw Myin Lat, Nu Mama (3), Tarahkaw Sara (6) hte Jubilee Missa hkungga sa shanglawm ai poi sa masha 300 daram rai nga ai.
